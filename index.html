who is gulaabi art?

Gulaabi.Art, aka Lavleen Kaur, is a digital artist and a student at the University of Toronto. She is currently studying mathematics, computer science, and professional writing.

Lavleen focuses on bringing nostalgia to life, with many of her works featuring familiar characters from television, film, and comics. She is also a lover of fashion and visual aesthetics.

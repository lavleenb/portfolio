ariana grande oscars look (2025)

this piece was inspired by ariana grande's schiaparelli gown worn to the oscars red carpet. i loved the structural shape of the skirt, and it's probably one of my favourites from her wicked press tour.

this piece was made using procreate on my ipad pro.

charliXCX (2024)

this piece features a semi-realistic portrait of music artist Charli XCX during her Brat album tour. the lyrics behind her are from the song 'girl, so confusing' featuring Lorde. I was inspired by Charli's approach to fashion during this album era, as it was very different from what she's done in the past.

this piece was made using procreate on my ipad pro.

glitzy sabrina (2025)

i was inspired to recreate this sparkly sequined dress that Sabrina Carpenter wore during her Short n' Sweet tour. in developing my personal art style, i've been enjoying drawing the hair with a different, more cartoon-ish style, making it appear as if it is floating on top of her head.

this piece was made using procreate on my ipad pro.

lavender (2024)

this piece was made for a close friend of mine as a housewarming gift. the goal was to create a simple elegant illustration to match her space, and the embroidery floss bow adds practicality and a tangible element.

this piece was made using watercolour paints on watercolour paper.

hydrangeas (2024)

i was inspired to recreate this sparkly sequined dress that Sabrina Carpenter wore during her Short n' Sweet tour. in developing my personal art style, i've been enjoying drawing the hair with a different, more cartoon-ish style, making it appear as if it is floating on top of her head.

this piece was made using procreate on my ipad pro.

philodendron (2024)

i wanted to create a portrait of my philodendron, michaelangelo, and try adding a tactile element to the painting. i attempted a hanging plant holder with jute, although i don't fully love the outcome. i'm sure michaelangelo loves his portrait though.

this piece was made using acrylics and jute on canvas.

H2O moodboards: cleo sertori

i created three moodboards for each of the main protagonists of the show "H2O: Just Add Water", a series i enjoyed watching as a kid. this one features the character Cleo Sertori, with three digital portraits of the character, and decorated with various stickers representing her.

this piece was made using procreate and canva.

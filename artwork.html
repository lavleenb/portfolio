ariana grande oscars look

(2025, digital)


this piece was inspired by ariana grande's schiaparelli gown worn to the oscars red carpet. i loved the structural shape of the skirt, and it's probably one of my favourites from her wicked press tour.

charli XCX

(2024, digital)


this artwork is a portrait of artist Charli XCX during the release and promotion of her album "brat". the background features lyrics from the song "girl, so confusing", featuring lorde.

glitzy sabrina

(2025, digital)


an attempt to re-create sabrina carpenter's sparkly sequin dress in my artstyle.

lavender

(2024, watercolour)


a watercolour study of lavender flowers, with a matching string to help hang the image.

hydrangeas

(2024, acrylic and ribbon on canvas)


a study on hydrangea flowers with acrylic paint. i really wanted to get the illusion of glass and liquid water right with this study. garnished with a ribbon bow.

philodendron

(2024, acrylic and jute on canvas)


inspired by my philodendron, michaelangelo, i painted this piece using acrylics with the intention of adding some kind of tactile element to it. using jute string, i attempted to create a macrame-like plant hanging, although i'm partial to how it came out. i think mike is fond of his portrait, though.
